Frequently Asked Questions

My app is crashing, what should I do?

Please try restarting the app or your device. If the issue persists, check for updates in the App Store.

How do I restore purchases?

Go to the app settings and tap on "Restore Purchases". Ensure you are logged in with the same ID used to make the purchase.
