For the free versions of our applications, we may use third-party advertising partners to serve ads. To deliver relevant and personalized advertisements, we may collect and use certain identifiers:

You can control personalized advertising by adjusting the settings on your device (e.g., "Ask App Not to Track" on iOS or opting out of personalized ads in Android settings).
